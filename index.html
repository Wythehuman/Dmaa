About Department of Marine Adminstration

Department of Marine Administration is one of the Departments of Ministry of Transport in Myanmar. The present day Department of Marine Administration was founded since the war days in 1930 and called Principal Port Officer.

This is the officially presentative site for accessing any information related with Myanmar seafarer registration, verification and seaferer related information services.
